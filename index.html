Starter Guide

Create 3 Accounts.
Main Account: This account focus on PVE & PVP.
Mining/Fishing Account (Alt Accounts): This account focus on AFK Farming on Mining or Fishing.
Login your main account.
Choose any job/class and put stats atleast on STR (500).
Claim the freebies on the following NPC:
Solo Package
Admin Solo Package
Season 2 Compensation
First - Last Month Reward
Claim the rewards at Monthsary Reward NPC.

In-game Commands

To access in-game commands, you must type @commands
To use the commands, you must include @ at the beginning of the command (Ex. @rates).
COMMANDS DESCRIPTION
rates Gives you the rates of the server.
refresh Refresh your screen in-game.
autotrade To autovend your character.
pubclose To close the pub that is created using the @pub.
iteminfo/ii Show information of the item (Ex. @ii apple).
storage Access the storage without using the kafra.
breakguild To break your created guild.
warp Warp you on random places of the designated map (Ex. @warp prontera).
guildlvl/glvl Level up your guild (Ex @guildlvl 50/@glvl 50).
ignoredrop Ignore/hide items drop on the floor.
autoloottype/aloottype Automatically loot the base on type (Ex @aloottype +6).
go Warp you to the city (Ex. @go 0/@go prontera).
alootid Automatically loot the item based on id/name of that item (Ex. @alootid +512/@alootid +apple).
time Show you the server time.
changegm Change your guild master.
resist Shows information regarding on the resist of your character.
accept Accept duel invitation.
whereis Shows the location where your can see the particular mob (Ex. @whereis poring).
whodrops Shows which mob drops the designated item (Ex. @whodrops apple).
allskill/skillall Learn & Level up all your job skill to max level (Except Platinum Skill).
storeall Store all your items, including your equipments to storage.
guildstorage/gstorage Access your guild storage.
hideview Hide players equipment (Same behavior of nohat grf).
hominfo Shows information of your homunculus.
homevolution Evolve your homunculus.
invite Invite player to a duel (You can use it when @duel is created).
packetfilter Shows packetfiltering usage.
commands Shows all the commands & script-bound commands.
jailtime Shows your remaining time when jailed.
homlevel Level up your homunculus.
request Send message to online GM.
autoloot Automatically loot all items drop by monster based on percentage (Ex. @autoloot 5 = all item with droprate of 5% & below will be automatically looted).
duel Create a duel.
pub Create a clone pub (Ex. @pub "test" "test").
mobinfo/mi Shows information of the designated mob/monster (Ex. @mobinfo poring/@mi poring).
showview Shows the hidden equipments of all players (Counterpart of hideview).
SCRIPT-BOUND COMMANDS DESCRIPTION
mission Show your mission progress.
vip1 Use your Emergency Buff Badge v1.
checkpoints Check all your points.
gotm Warp you to gotm map.
joinporingcatcher Join you to poring catcher event.
vip2 Use your Emergency Buff Badge v2.
todevilsquare Join you to devilsquare event.
lootfragment Loot all fragments (Thantaos tower).
joindice Join you to dice event.
joinsabong Join you to sabong event.
betsabong Input your bet for sabong event.
ii2 N/A.
itemdesc N/A.
tosurvival Return you to survival area/map.
resu Ressurect your character (Consume Token of Siegfried).
lootxmasball Automatically loot xmas ball (During Xmas Event).
lootmvp Automatically loot MVP Drop (All MVP Drops needed for Mission).
exit Allows you to exit map (Maps where @warp/@go is not authorized) and will return you to your savepoint.
npc Warp you to designated NPC.
shout Broadcast your message in whole server.
limiteditem2 Shows items that have restriction in designated map (Ex. @limiteditem2 prontera).
npclist Show the npclist.
guide Same as npclist.
guides Same as npclist.
renamecheck N/A.
vvip Access vvip map.
limiteditem1 N/A.
vvip3 Use your VVIP Blessing Beads.
joinkoe Join you to KOE.
woe Join/Warp you to WOE.
woetime Shows you the schedule of WOE.
hold Hold your position.
limiteditem N/A.
endlesstower Warp you to endlesstower stone.

Guides

Below are recommended setups for Starter, Average, High, and Final gears. These setups are mostly used on LHZ 1 & 2 MVP Hunt, Endlesstower, and NYD.

  1. Starter

    Considered as newbie on the server.

  2. Average

    Upgraded version of Starter. Next step for Starter.

  3. High

    Upgraded version of Average. Must have RSX-0806 Deposited. If experience damage bug (too much damage), considered hp or reduce on demi-human on cards.

  4. Final

    Considered as endgame on the server. If experience damage bug (too much damage), considered hp or reduce on demi-human on cards.
